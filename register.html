How to Apply

Welcome to the NABS Admissions Process! We are excited to guide you through the steps to become a part of our vibrant and nurturing community. Our application process is designed to be straightforward and comprehensive, ensuring that we get to know each prospective student and their unique potential..

  1. Obtain the Admission Form: Download the admission form from our website or collect it from the school office.

  2. Complete the Form: Fill out the form with accurate information about your child. Ensure all sections are completed, including child's name, date of birth, former school, and health information.

  3. Attach Necessary Documents: Attach a recent photograph and any other required documents as mentioned in the form.

  4. Pay the Registration Fee: Submit the non-refundable registration fee along with the completed form.

  5. Submit the Form: Submit the filled form and attached documents to the school office. You can also send them via mail to the address provided in the form.

  6. Entrance Exam: Your child may need to take an entrance exam. The date and details will be communicated to you upon form submission.

  7. Notification of Admission: After the entrance exam, you will be notified about the admission status and class placement of your child.

For any inquiries or assistance, please contact our admission office at +233 239 098 2478 or email us at info@naturalbrainsacademy.com.
