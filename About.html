About Us

Welcome to Natural rains Academy

Natural Brains Academy had humble beginnings as special evening classes where I personally moved
from house to house, teaching children. Over a decade, from 1993 to 2003, I visited more than 70 homes
in areas such as Abeka, Fadama, North Kaneshie, Tesano, Akweteman, Apenkwa, and Nii Boi Town. This grassroots approach allowed me to provide personalized after-school lessons, fostering a love for learning
among countless children.

The students I taught came from a diverse array of schools, including Soul Clinic International School,
St. Theresa Preparatory, Prince of Peace International School, Zealous Preparatory School, Harrow
International, St. Thomas Preparatory, Roman Basic School, St. Augustine Preparatory, Apenkwa
Basic School, St. Lawrence Preparatory School, Achimota Primary and Preparatory Schools, Tesano Basic
School, Cosmos School, and Majomar Academy. It has been incredibly rewarding to see many of these students grow up to become successful professionals in various fields.

Welcome to Natural Brains Academy

In a significant development, on September 13, 2006, my wife and I took a leap of faith and established a permanent school at Kpobiman. The local chief and his elders graciously welcomed us, recognizing the need for educational opportunities in the area, as there were no other schools at that time. On our first day, we enrolled two children, Clifford Osei and Thomas Quaye. The very next day, September 14, 2006, Joseph Wornu became our third student, marking the end of an auspicious first week.

To our delight, by the end of the following week, enrollment had surged to 20 children. This rapid increase was a clear indication of the community's eagerness for quality education and their trust in our vision.

Since those early days, Natural Brains Academy has continued to grow and evolve. Our commitment to providing an excellent education has only strengthened. We now offer a wide range of academic and extracurricular programs designed to nurture the intellectual and personal growth of our students. Our faculty comprises dedicated educators who are passionate about teaching and fostering a positive learning environment.

Our alumni network is a source of immense pride, with former students achieving remarkable success in various professions. This enduring impact on our students' lives is a testament to the foundational principles of dedication, personalized attention, and community engagement that Natural Brains Academy was built upon.

Looking ahead, we are excited to continue our journey of educational excellence, expanding our reach and enhancing our offerings to ensure that every child in our care can achieve their full potential. The story of Natural Brains Academy is one of perseverance, community support, and unwavering commitment to the transformative power of education
